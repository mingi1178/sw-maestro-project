troubleshoot Weak Point Analysis

"...set it up as a single instance first..."

Single Point of Failure (SPOF)

Proposing a single instance in a distributed systems interview is a red flag. You must address high availability and fault tolerance from the start, mentioning concepts like clustering, sharding, or leader-follower setups.

"...just set a short TTL on everything..."

Inadequate Consistency Strategy

Relying solely on TTL for consistency is insufficient for critical data. Discuss cache invalidation strategies (e.g., write-through, write-around, cache-aside) and how to handle race conditions during updates.
